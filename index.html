Who are we?

The Board Game Guardians is a board game club located in Preston, Lancashire. Our members come together to enjoy a wide variety of games, from strategy classics to casual, cooperative fun. At the heart of our club is a commitment to safeguarding and ensuring everyone feels welcome, respected, and protected.

What do we do?

We organize regular game nights where players can connect, socialize, and explore new board games. Alongside the fun, we actively raise awareness about community safeguarding through workshops and discussions, educating members on how to create a safe and inclusive space for all.

What is safeguarding?

Safeguarding is the act of protecting people's health, human rights, and wellbeing, and ensuring they are free from abuse, neglect, and harm. It's a collective responsibility to safeguard children, young people, and adults.
